MadScience Experiments

by Naresh Raheja June 25, 2026

Simple Agentic Flow

1

Input Guardrail

Checks that the goal is a classroom experiment and not just a broad topic.

2

Scientist Agent

Creates the objective, hypothesis, materials, method, learning outcomes, and cost.

3

Safety Officer Agent

Reviews hazards and can approve, request changes, reject, or escalate.

4

Budget Analyst Agent

Compares the cost with editable classroom budget limits.

5

Orchestrator

Combines reviews without overriding safety or budget decisions.

6

Audit Trail

Shows the proposal, reviews, final decision, and explanation.

Input Water quality and radish seed growth
Guardrail Valid classroom experiment
Review Household materials and cost within limit
Decision APPROVED with audit trail

Iterative Changes

1

From Scripted to Flexible

Moved beyond fixed demo cases so new goals keep their own topic and proposal language.

2

Budget Became Configurable

Added cost input and editable limits: approve, modify, or reject based on presenter thresholds.

3

Safety Guardrails Expanded

Rejected high voltage, hazardous materials, biological agents, explosives, and high heat.

4

Logic Check Added

Marked non-actionable goals, such as planet trajectory studies, invalid before agent review.

5

Classroom Safety Tuned

Ordinary seed growth experiments now approve; outdoor samples and unknown materials still require controls.
